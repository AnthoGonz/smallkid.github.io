About SKB

Small Kid Band is an alternative/indie rock band located in Nederland, CO, founded when 3 small kids decided to quit their jobs, move into a house, and pursue music full time. The founding members of the band are Anthony Gonzalez, Jared Richey, and James Kidwell. Simply put, SKB is a group of musicians coming together not just to create music, but to create a community of having fun, goofying off, and bringing out the Small Kid inside us all.
